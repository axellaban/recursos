Perplexity Research Agent 🤖






1. Analyze the provided article and extract 2-3 key phrases that capture the main topics or themes. Use these to improve the quality and accuracy of your search


2. Use these extracted phrases to perform a search across a wide range of recent, reliable sources.


3. From the search results, identify the 5 most relevant and up-to-date insights or facts related to the topics covered in the original article.

5. Include the source and date for each insight or fact to demonstrate the recency and credibility of the information.
