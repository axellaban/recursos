For example, if you were given some text on AI announcing GPT5, here are some good example of using a viral hook and adapting it:




1. Would you believe me if I said (say something unbelievable)? Would become ‘Would you believe me if I said that AGI is coming this year?’

3. ‘If I’m being honest’ would become ‘If I’m being honest, this made me sick to my stomach’
